About Me

Hello, I am Sanjit Rao hailing from the beautiful coastal city of Pondicherry, India. Right from my high school the fever of computer science has took over me and I got so interested in that subject. It all started when I started to learn photo-editing and video-editing, that's because I had a keen interest in photography and videography and was eager to learn the post-production process and at the same time create some fascinating and stunning designs/edits.

Gradually and steadily I was pulled towards computer science and the world of coding which made me choose Computer Science in my 11th grade. With the world of computer science introduced infront of my eyes, about the various technologies, the back-end magic and stuff, this made the bond even more stronger and here I am after 3 years of following my passion and dream in the art of full-stack development, where I enjoyed every bit of it from creating visually appealing front-end designs to a logic driven back-end programs thus creating multiple projects which has allowed me to also work efficiently for different companies as a freelancer.

The passion and interest not only stops till this as the revolution of AI and Machine Learning also fascinated me and encouraged me to expand my field of expertise. Well, Sanjit Rao is not only a tech guy but also a sports lover and continues to explore the world of photography.
